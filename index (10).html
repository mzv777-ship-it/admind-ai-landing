Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 28, 2026

AdMind AI is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our Google Ads API and Google Analytics integration tool.

1. Information We Access

  • Google Ads: campaigns, ad groups, keywords, search terms, bids, budgets, Quality Scores
  • Google Analytics 4: sessions, conversions, user behavior, traffic sources, funnel data
  • Performance metrics: impressions, clicks, CTR, CPC, ROAS, CPA
  • Auction Insights: competitor impression share and overlap data

2. How We Use This Information

We use data exclusively to provide automated campaign optimization, write ad copy, generate reports, and deliver performance insights. We do not sell, share, or transfer your data to any third parties.

AdMind AI processes data in real-time and does not permanently store your Google Ads or GA4 raw data. OAuth 2.0 tokens are encrypted and never exposed or shared.

4. Google API Services User Data Policy

AdMind AI's use of information from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy , including the Limited Use requirements.
